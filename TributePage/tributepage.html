Pokemon Evolution

The timeline of Pokemon and all of the generations

The following is the timeline of Pokemon Generations:

  • 1996 - 1990
    Generation I: Kanto
  • 1999 - 2002
    Generation II: Johto
  • 2002 - 2006
    Generation III: Hoenn
  • 2006 - 2010
    Generation IV: Sinnoh
  • 2010-2013
    Generation V: Unova
  • 2013 - 2016
    Generation VI: Kalos
  • 2016 - 2019
    Generation VII: Alola
  • 2019 - 2022
    Generation VIII: Galar
  • 2022 - Now
    Generation IX: Unova
